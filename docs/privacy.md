# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: {new Date().toLocaleDateString()}_

TchueKAM is built privacy-first. This policy explains exactly what data we touch and what we don't.

## What stays on your machine
- Your conversations
- Your settings and preferences
- Workspace indexes
- All files you read, edit, or create with TchueKAM

These never leave your computer unless you explicitly enable a cloud feature.

## What we collect (only with your permission)

### Anonymous telemetry (default: on, opt-out anytime)
- App version and operating system
- Feature usage counts (e.g. "voice input used 3 times this week")
- Crash reports (stack traces, no conversation content)

You can turn this off at **Settings → Privacy → Telemetry**.

### Conversation sync (default: off)
If you opt in, conversations are encrypted on your device and stored in your account on our servers. You can disable this anytime and delete cloud copies from **Settings → Privacy → Sync**.

## What we never collect
- The text of your conversations (unless sync is on, and even then it's encrypted)
- File contents
- Keystroke logs
- Browsing activity outside TchueKAM
- Anything that could identify you personally (without explicit account creation)

## Third parties
- **GitHub** — TchueKAM downloads updates from GitHub Releases. GitHub may log your IP when you download. See [GitHub's privacy policy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement).
- **PostHog** — anonymous product analytics (only if telemetry is on)

We don't sell, share, or expose your data to advertisers. Ever.

## Your rights
- **Export** — download everything we have about you in JSON format from **Settings → Privacy → Export my data**
- **Delete** — wipe all local data via **Settings → Privacy → Delete everything**
- **Cloud delete** — if you enabled sync, **Settings → Privacy → Delete cloud account** removes everything from our servers within 30 days

## Children's privacy
TchueKAM is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, [contact us](contact) and we'll delete it.

## Changes to this policy
We'll update this page when our practices change. Material changes will be announced in the app and on the [website](https://tchuekam.com).

## Contact
