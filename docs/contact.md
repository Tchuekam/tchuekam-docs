# Contact

We read every message.

## Email
**[support@tchuekam.com](mailto:support@tchuekam.com)** — for all questions, bug reports, and feedback.

## Book a consultation
Want to talk through how TchueKAM fits your workflow? [Book a free 45-minute session](https://tchuekam.com#booking) on the main site.

## Response time
- **General questions:** 1–2 business days
- **Bug reports with logs:** 1 business day
- **Critical issues (data loss, security):** Same day

## Reporting bugs

When emailing about a bug, please include:

1. **TchueKAM version** — find this in Settings → About
2. **Operating system and version** — e.g. "Windows 11", "macOS 14 Sonoma"
3. **What you were doing** — step-by-step
4. **What happened vs. what you expected**
5. **Logs** — attach the relevant log file from:
   - Windows: `%AppData%\TchueKAM\logs\`
   - macOS: `~/Library/Logs/TchueKAM/`
   - Linux: `~/.config/TchueKAM/logs/`

## Security disclosures
If you've found a security vulnerability, please email **[support@tchuekam.com](mailto:support@tchuekam.com)** with **[SECURITY]** in the subject line. We'll respond within 24 hours and work with you on responsible disclosure.

## Business inquiries
For partnerships, press, or commercial questions, email **[support@tchuekam.com](mailto:support@tchuekam.com)** with **[BUSINESS]** in the subject line.
